Who We Are

Accountable Tracker is a product of Trident Visual LLC, a California-based company. "We," "us," and "our" refer to Trident Visual LLC. Contact us at support@accountabletracker.com.

What We Collect

  • Account info — name and email via Google OAuth sign-in
  • Business profile — company name, logo, vehicle info, and onboarding details you provide
  • Expense data — home office, mileage, phone/internet, and out-of-pocket expenses you log
  • Billing info — subscription status via Stripe (we never store your card details)
  • Usage data — basic app activity for troubleshooting and improvement

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the service: displaying expense reports, processing billing, sending transactional emails, and fixing bugs. We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth — authentication, governed by Google's privacy policy
  • Stripe — payment processing; Stripe stores your payment method securely
  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and DNS; processes request metadata in transit
  • Gmail — transactional email delivery from our support address

CPA Accounts

CPA portal users may access expense data for clients who have accepted their invitation. CPA users are responsible for handling client data in accordance with their professional obligations. We do not grant access beyond what the client has explicitly consented to.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on servers in the United States with encrypted HTTPS access and daily encrypted backups. No system is perfectly secure — use the app accordingly.

Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained while your account is active. To delete your account and associated data, email support@accountabletracker.com. We will remove your data within 30 days.

Your Rights

You may request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting support@accountabletracker.com.

Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be communicated by email to active subscribers before taking effect. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
