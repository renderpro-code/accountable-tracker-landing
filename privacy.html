Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026  ·  Trident Visual LLC

Not financial or tax advice. Accountable Tracker is a software tool designed to help you organize and document reimbursable business expenses. It does not provide financial, tax, accounting, or legal advice. Always consult a qualified CPA or tax professional for guidance on IRS compliance and accountable plan requirements.

Who We Are

Accountable Tracker is a product of Trident Visual LLC, a California-based company. References to "we," "us," or "our" throughout this policy refer to Trident Visual LLC. You can reach us at support@accountabletracker.com.

What We Collect

When you use Accountable Tracker, we collect the following:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided via Google OAuth when you sign in
  • Business profile data — company name, logo, vehicle information, and other details you enter during onboarding
  • Expense data — home office, mileage, phone/internet, and out-of-pocket expenses you log in the app
  • Billing information — payment method and subscription status, processed by Stripe (we do not store your card details)
  • Usage data — basic app activity for troubleshooting and improving the service

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Accountable Tracker:

  • To provide and display your expense reports and PDF exports
  • To process subscription billing through Stripe
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmations, billing receipts, trial reminders)
  • To troubleshoot bugs and improve app reliability

We do not sell your data. We do not use your expense data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in authentication. Governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — for payment processing and subscription management. Stripe stores your payment method. We never see your full card number.
  • Cloudflare — for hosting, CDN, and DNS. Cloudflare may process request metadata as traffic passes through their network.
  • Gmail (Google) — for sending transactional emails from our support address.

CPA Accounts

If you are a CPA or accounting professional using the CPA portal, you may have access to expense reports belonging to clients who have accepted your invitation. You are responsible for handling that client data in accordance with your professional obligations and applicable law. We do not grant CPA accounts access to client data beyond what the client has consented to by accepting your invitation.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers located in the United States. We use Cloudflare Tunnels for encrypted HTTPS access and maintain daily encrypted backups. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is perfectly secure — use the app accordingly.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and wish to delete your account and associated data, email support@accountabletracker.com and we will remove your data within 30 days.

Your Rights

You may request a copy of your data, correction of inaccurate data, or deletion of your account at any time by contacting support@accountabletracker.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email to active subscribers. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
